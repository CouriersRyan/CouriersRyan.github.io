Antiquitus

[ Ryan Luke Zhang Home ]

  • A physical collection building eurogame created in a team of 4. By Cole Conrad Shrader, Jenny Cheng, Thomas Tang, and Ryan Zhang.
    My contributions to this project was in serving as the team's producer throughout our 6 month production, ensuring timely completion and scheduling of various tasks and following up on missed deadlines, communicating and coming up with a schedule based on our progress.
    I also served as the team's graphic designer, making the visuals for the rulebook and the placards (the cards with text, NOT the tiles). Focused on incorporating the other artist's work as well as focusing on communicating the rules as best as possible.
